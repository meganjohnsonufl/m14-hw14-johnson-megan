About

Hi! My name is Megan and this is my little slice of the Internet. I am a current graduate student at the University of Florida working towards my Master's of Arts in Mass Communications with a concentration in Web Design. I completed my undergrad in 2015 through Robert Morris University, graduating with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Media Arts with a concentration in Graphic Design and a minor in Photography. I am from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania and still reside there today. I'm truly a Steel City Yinzer as they say. I also have a certification in UX/UI Design from Parsons The New School out of New York City, NY. I have had the privilege to work in hospitality and tourism, higher education, advertising agencies, and freelancing. Always up to learning something new, there is always something going on in the design world. My career goals include becoming a senior graphic designer or a UX designer. I want to go further in my career and become more than what I am right now.

Outside of work, I have one horse. She is 27 years old and is a registered Quarter Horse. I've been involved with horses for 20 years now and have no plans on stopping. I've had horses for 17 of those years. I am also an avid photographer. It's a hobby, but also a profession for me. I have my own photography business where I specialize in dog sports, pets, weddings, and cosplay photography. Click the button below to see some of my latest work.
